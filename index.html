About Us

Welcome to Oxford International Academy

Know About Us

The Oxford International Academy Was Founded by SHREE POORA RAM MEMORIAL SANSTHAN, Panchyawala on 5th April 2010. Its founder Mr. Moti Ram Chopra started the school with his vision to impart value education in a compassionate environment that triggers their sense towards natural learning. With a teaching staff of 46 and an overall staff of 60, each member of the Oxford family is unique in their own way and gives their heart and soul to the cause of learning for the students. The school has an association with health and welfare forums and counselors for ensuring the holistic health of the school children. Unparallel state of the art facilities, the school campus has effective classrooms with projectors and latest computers to implement its interactive learning programs and innovative curriculum. The school encourages both indoor and outdoor sports like Cricket, Football, Chess, Carom and many more with its vast lush green playground and well-resourced sports With the activity-based learning, sports, and co-curricular activities, young minds get 360-degree exposure and opportunity to tap their true potential. The extensive and well-stacked library helps the students to increase their knowledge base. The ultra-modern laboratories have all the latest equipment and technology to feed a child's curiosity. The CBSE-affiliated school strictly adheres to the CCE pattern of learning. The school also emphasizes training in the field of art and craft, dance, music- vocal and instrumental, martial arts, yoga, and meditation. The school, known for its discipline, Systematic functioning, and well-cultured environment, proves itself every year by getting assorted feathers in its cap with its students making marks in both academic and sports arenas. The comfortable and child-friendly surroundings start with a child's journey to reach the building with a well-placed transportation facility.
